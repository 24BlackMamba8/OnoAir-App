User Guide

Welcome to the OnoAir user guide! Here you'll find comprehensive information on how to use our system to book flights, manage your reservations, and more.

Booking a Flight

  1. Go to the "Book a Flight" page.
  2. Enter your departure and arrival cities.
  3. Select your travel dates.
  4. Choose your preferred flight from the available options.
  5. Enter your passenger details and confirm your booking.

Managing Your Reservations

To manage your existing reservations, you can:

  • View your upcoming flights.
  • Change your flight details (if allowed).
  • Cancel your booking (if allowed).

Other Features

Our system also offers the following features:

  • View last-minute flight deals.
  • Contact our customer support team for assistance.
  • Manage your user profile and preferences.
